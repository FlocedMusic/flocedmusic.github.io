About

Dark, tense and nostalgic electronic music.

FLOCED is a music project shaped by electronic music, post-punk, rock, synthwave, coldwave and electro dance. Between nocturnal atmospheres, mechanical rhythms and tense melodies, each track explores a world where raw energy meets melancholy.

Between shadow and light, past and future, FLOCED moves like an image revealed in the dark: intense, cinematic and deeply evocative.

Extension / Floced FX

Floced-FX for Ableton Live.

Ableton Extensions, available since Live 12.4.5, allow users to run additional tools inside Ableton Live through the Extensions SDK.

Extensions can interact with tracks, clips, MIDI, devices, tempo and other parts of a Live Set to automate creative tasks.
